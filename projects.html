Same Level Prototype:

Description: This is a project made early 2021 when I was learning some new skills in game development. The game is simply the same level but with many twists. What this entails is the first level repeating over and over but each time a new change happens. This change can range from inverted controls, player sizes, reduce visibility and many more. The game is perfect for speedrunning but can also be played casually.

Spring Time Sunflower (Game Jam Edition)

Description: A game made in less than 24 hours due to me joining the Spring Jam 2021 a bit late. All parts of this game were done by me, including the music and graphics. The game involves moving your sunflower left and right, collecting water but avoiding salt. Once your flower grows to a certain size, you have the opportunity to retire that flower to the fields and begin anew, resetting your score but gaining a multiplier boost. It has a very high risk/reward system despite it's simplistic gameplay and it's certainly a fun way to kill some time, trying to get absurd scores
